Data Science Methods and Practices Coursework

Aim: To design a high-level data science research project. The portfolio must demonstrate your ability to critically evaluate the current state of a field, identify a technical gap, and propose a rigorous, ethical, and feasible project plan.

Theme: Time Series Analysis 

Research Paper Title: Towards Clinically Meaningful Explainability in ICU Time Series Prediction Models

Research Gap: Current explainability methods such as SHAP and LIME are not inherently designed for time series data and fail to capture temporal dependencies, resulting in explanations that lack temporal coherence and are not clinically interpretable in ICU decision-making contexts.

Research Question: How can explainability methods be adapted or enhanced to capture temporal dependencies and provide clinically meaningful interpretations for ICU time series prediction models?

Objectives:

1. To critically evaluate existing explainability methods (e.g., SHAP, LIME, and attention-based approaches) applied to deep learning models for ICU time series prediction.

2. To analyse the limitations of these methods in capturing temporal dependencies and producing consistent and clinically meaningful explanations.

3. To develop a conceptual framework for temporally-aware explainability, incorporating the importance of feature contributions across time steps.

4. To define appropriate evaluation criteria for assessing the quality of explanations, including faithfulness, stability, and clinical interpretability.
